About Our Cafe

Welcome to Hotel Lanka Super Corals, where luxury meets wellness in the heart of Sri Lanka's breathtaking coast. Our hotel is committed to providing a rejuvenating experience for our guests, featuring elegantly designed rooms that offer panoramic views of the azure waters. Each accommodation blends modern comfort with traditional Sri Lankan aesthetics, ensuring each stay is both relaxing and culturally enriching.

Our unique emphasis on health includes exclusive access to premium alkaline water, known for its numerous benefits, helping you feel refreshed and revitalized throughout your visit. At Hotel Lanka Super Corals, your adventure awaits beyond your room. Dive into a world of vibrant coral reefs, indulge in water sports, or explore the rich heritage of our local culture with curated excursions. Savor the exquisite flavors of our on-site dining options, emphasizing healthy and delicious menu selections complemented by our alkaline water.
